GDD Abstract

Mini Champs is a Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA) and Tower Defense (TD) hybrid indie game that focuses on mixing casual gameplay with complex and extensive strategy, skill expressive and fun-to-play champions with deep lore, fun and vast personalities.

Using the Mini Champ's extensive and engaging abilities and characteristics, recruitaable allies to aid and assist them, and equipment which increase their traits even more. The game focuses on a champion character, the Mini Champ, and their battle to defend their Crystal against enemies and massive bosses summoned by the Portal.

The game takes inspiration from well-known and genre defining strategy games such as Dota 2 and League of Legends (LoL), and action packed indie games such as the Bloons franchise, Dead Cells, and more.

This game design document is an extensive document that details and highlights the entire game design and development process of Mini Champs.

Introduction

Mini Champs is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) and tower defense hybrid game where a player-controlled character, called the Mini Champs. Defends their crystal from enemies rushing to it from three or more lanes. The player can move their chosen Mini Champs around and kill enemies, deploy, and buff deployed allies.
